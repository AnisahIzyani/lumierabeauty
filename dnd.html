📍SKINCARE CAN'T MIX




VITAMIN C PLUS AHAS/BHAS OR MATRIXYL

➡VITAMIN C SHOULD NOT BE MIXED WITH AHA/BHA OR MATRIXYL BECAUSE IT CAN CAUSE SKIN IRRITATION AND REDUCE THE EFFECTIVENESS OF THE PRODUCTS



GLYCOLIC ACID PLUS LACTIC ACID OR SALICYLIC ACID

➡GLYCOLIC ACID CANNOT BE MIXED WITH LACTIC ACID OR SALICYLIC ACID BECAUSE IT CAN CAUSE OVER EXFOLIATION AND SKIN IRRITATION



RETINOL PLUS VITAMIN C OR SALICYLIC ACID

➡RETINOL CANNOT BE MIXED WITH VITAMIN C OR SALICYLIC ACID BECAUSE THEY CAN REDUCE ITS EFFECTIVENESS AND CAUSE SKIN IRRITATION
